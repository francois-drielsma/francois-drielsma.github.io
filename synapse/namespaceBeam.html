enum Beam::PhaseSpaceVariable

Enumerates the phase space variables.

The axes are set up so that the axis points downstream, the axis point upwards from the ground and the axis follows the right hand convetion, i.e. . Coordinates :

  • x: position along the transverse axis expressed in [mm]
  • x: position along the transverse axis expressed in [mm]
  • px: momentum along the transverse axis expressed in [MeV/c]
  • py: momentum along the transverse axis expressed in [MeV/c]
  • pz: momentum along the longitudinal axis expressed in [MeV/c]
enum Beam::SumStat

Enumerates the types of summary statistics of a beam bunch.

An enumeration allows to distinguish between different summary statistics

  • alpha: Twiss parameter alpha
  • beta: Twiss parameter beta
  • gamma: Twiss parameter gamma
  • mecl: Mechanical angular momentum
  • eps: Geometric emittance
  • neps: Normalised emittance
  • trans: Tansmission
  • subeps: α-subemittance
  • amp: α-amplitude
  • vol: α-fractional emittance

mode: amplitude mode

  • mom: mean momentum
  • trans: transmission
