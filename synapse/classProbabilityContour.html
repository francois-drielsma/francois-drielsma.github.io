Detailed Description

Reconstructs the optimal α-contour of a Probability Density Function.

Provided with a scalar field normalised to 1 and defined everywhere, the algorithm finds the manifold that bounds the α-contour and reconstructs its volume. The α-contour is the smallest hypersurface that contains a fraction α of the total probability.
