Detailed Description

Holds a bunch of particles at a specific position along the beam line.

It stores the array of phase space vectors of all the particle that are contained in the bunch.

Quantities associated with the bunch such as Twiss parameters, amplitudes, emittance, volume, etc. may be computed and stored here. This avoids unnecessarily repeating operations such as calculating the covariance matrix.

It is designed to hold a single species of particles.
