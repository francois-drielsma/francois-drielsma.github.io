Detailed Description

Reweights the MAUS simulation to reproduce the amplitude distribution observed in data.

Algorithm that reweights the MAUS simulation to faithfully reproduce the amplitude distribution observed in hte corresponding data downstream of the MICE absorber. It uses the requested density estimator to reconstruct the minimum local density as a function of the fraction of the beam included.
