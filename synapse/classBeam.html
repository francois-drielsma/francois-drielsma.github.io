Detailed Description

Holds a beam ensemble at a specific position along the beam line.

It stores the array of phase space vectors of all the particle that are contained in the beam ensemble.

Quantities associated with the beam such as Twiss parameters, amplitudes, emittance, volume, etc. may be computed and stored here. This avoids unnecessarily repeating operations such as calculating the covariance matrix.

It is designed to hold single specifies of particles.
