Detailed Description

Holds a bunch of particles at a specific position along the beam line.

Quantities associated with the bunch such as Twiss parameters, amplitudes, emittance, volume, etc. may be computed and stored here. This avoids unnecessarily repeating operations such as computing the particle amplitudes.

It is designed to hold a single species of particles.
